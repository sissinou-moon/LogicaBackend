## Xiaomi Mimo V2 Flash Guide

### Overview
- **Xiaomi Mimo V2** is a compact smart home device.
- It supports **Wi-Fi 6** and **Bluetooth 5.3**.
- Designed for **IoT connectivity** and **voice assistant integration**.

### Key Features
- **Processor**: Dual-core ARM Cortex-A53.
- **Memory**: 512MB RAM, 4GB eMMC storage.
- **Connectivity**: 2.4GHz/5GHz Wi-Fi, Bluetooth LE.
- **Audio**: Built-in microphone and speaker.

### Flashing Instructions
1. **Download Firmware**: Get the latest firmware from Xiaomi's official site.
2. **Prepare Device**: Ensure the device is powered and connected to Wi-Fi.
3. **Use Mi Flash Tool**: Open the tool and connect the device via USB.
4. **Select Firmware**: Choose the downloaded firmware file.
5. **Start Flashing**: Click 'Flash' and wait for completion.
6. **Reboot**: The device will restart automatically.

### Troubleshooting
- **Connection Issues**: Check USB cable and drivers.
- **Flash Failures**: Ensure firmware matches the device model.
- **Post-Flash Setup**: Reconnect to Wi-Fi and reconfigure settings.

### Safety Notes
- **Backup Data**: Flashing may erase settings.
- **Official Firmware Only**: Avoid third-party ROMs.
- **Warranty**: Flashing may void the warranty.

For more details, refer to Xiaomi's official documentation.
